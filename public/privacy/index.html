Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

LOTUS Painting & Maintenance Inc. (“Company”, “we”, “us”) operates the LOTUS Employee App (“App”) and the LOTUS customer messaging program. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and employee ID (for employees).
  • Time entries: Clock in/out timestamps, break durations, and work notes (for employees).
  • Business contact information: Name, email, phone number, and role for client property managers and technicians we do business with.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data: GPS coordinates are collected only while employees are clocked in to an active work shift. Location tracking stops when they clock out.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for app compatibility.

Information from Third Parties

  • Payroll data: Pay stubs, W-2 documents, and PTO balances are retrieved from Paychex Flex on the employee's behalf.
  • Work order data: Property assignments and work orders are retrieved from our internal systems and from our clients' systems via email and API.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Verify time and attendance at job sites
  • Process payroll and manage scheduling
  • Generate quotes, invoices, and manage work items for assigned properties
  • Communicate with clients about active work orders and service coordination
  • Provide employees access to their pay stubs and employment documents
  • Improve App functionality and fix technical issues

3. SMS & Messaging Program

We send SMS messages to our business clients (property managers, technicians, and other vendors) regarding active work orders, scheduling, and completion notifications. By providing your phone number as part of an active business relationship, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from us.

  • Message frequency varies based on active work orders.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages.
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at (910) 912-1349.
  • Phone numbers and message content are never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers: Supabase (data storage), Paychex (payroll processing), Intuit QuickBooks (accounting), Telnyx (SMS and voice), and Google (authentication and email) process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business operations: Company management accesses time, location, and work order data for payroll processing and job site verification.

5. Location Data

  • GPS location is collected only while employees are clocked in to an active work shift.
  • Location data is used to verify attendance at job sites.
  • Background location tracking activates at clock-in and stops at clock-out.
  • Employees can review their location data through the App.
  • Location data is stored securely and retained in accordance with employment record requirements.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication via secure tokens with automatic expiration
  • Rate limiting on login attempts
  • Input validation and sanitization on all data processing
  • Access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel

7. Data Retention

  • Time entries and location data: Retained for the duration of employment plus the period required by applicable labor laws.
  • Account information: Retained until account deactivation.
  • Message history: Retained for business records consistent with applicable law.
  • Payroll data: Managed by Paychex in accordance with their retention policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Opt out of SMS messaging at any time by replying STOP
  • Raise concerns about data handling with Company management

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for business use. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or other appropriate means. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina.
